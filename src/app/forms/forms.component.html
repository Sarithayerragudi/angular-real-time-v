Form Wizards

Form wizard examples in this page uses mat-horizontal-stepper` and` `mat-vertical-stepper`
components from Angular Material. You can get more information about them over here:

https://material.angular.io/components/stepper/overview.

We prepared couple examples to get you started. You are not limited with these examples, there are lots of options
to steppers and you can configure them to have different behaviors. Take a look at the official guide to learn more
about them.
